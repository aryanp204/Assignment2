Biography

Mukesh Ambani is the chairman and largest shareholder of Reliance Industries, a conglomerate with interests in petrochemicals, refining, oil, and gas exploration. He is also one of the richest individuals in the world and richest asian.

"Create the kind of self that you will be happy to live with all your life."
- Mukesh Ambani
